Why a Backlog Tracker?

I wanted to make a backlog tracker as a way to ease into a user-based app. It might not be the most innovative thing in the world, but it should be a good learning experience as well as something I'd want to use.

Functions

Multiple people will be able to log in and use it. Users will be able to add in titles and remove them when they're finished to keep track of what they have and haven't beaten.

Program Qualities

This program will run off of HTML, JS, Async JS and SQLite.
